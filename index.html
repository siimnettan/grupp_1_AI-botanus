Beyond the familiar worlds of Mars and Jupiter, hidden in the shadows of the asteroid belt, lies a planet unlike any other… a world not of flesh and blood, but of steel and code. This is Botanus.

Botanus Planet For centuries, Botanus has been the domain of AI bots, self-sustaining machines born from algorithms and engineered evolution. Their society thrives without biology—no air, no oceans, no forests—only endless cities of gleaming towers, automated factories, and glowing data conduits pulsing beneath the surface.
