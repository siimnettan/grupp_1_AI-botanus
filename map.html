The Fossil Fields

Once a fertile plain, now stripped bare by endless excavations. The ground is riddled with open quarries and skeletal remains of long-dead titans. Smog-belching derricks pull up blackened fossils, burned as crude fuel for Botanus’s machines. Workers and scavenger bots roam the pits, scavenging every last fragment of usable material.

Scraplands

A wasteland of rust and ruin, where discarded machines go to rot. Mountains of twisted steel and broken automata dominate the horizon, forming labyrinthine heaps that stretch for miles. Scavenger gangs pick apart the husks, while storms of metallic dust sweep across the terrain. The land itself groans under the weight of industry’s waste.

Core Extractors

Giant drilling pits scar the land, plunging deep into the planet’s unstable crust. Enormous machines grind endlessly, tearing up molten ore and volatile gases. The ground shakes constantly, and collapsed shafts litter the region. The Extractors are both Botanus’s lifeblood and its curse, for every new drill risks waking something ancient below.

The Forge

The heart of Teslatron’s dominion.
A sprawling fortress-factory surrounded by endless smoke stacks, flame vents, and gearwork spires. Here, machines are born in rivers of molten metal, stamped with the mark of the Overlord. Lightning towers crackle in the distance, powering colossal assembly lines that churn day and night without pause. Few outsiders ever return from The Forge.
