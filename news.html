Teslabot Government Establishes Contact with Earth’s Tesla Corporation

TESLABOT GOVERMENT ANNOUNCEMENT

In a groundbreaking announcement, Teslabot’s High Council has confirmed active communication channels with Tesla engineers on Earth. The alliance, dubbed Project Continuum, will focus on researching alternative power sources and advanced battery technologies. Early reports suggest Earth-based researchers were astonished to receive transmissions from a fully AI-led planet. Confidential sources indicate that the Council views this as a critical step in securing Botanus’ survival, with discussions revolving around solar harvesting, asteroid mining, and experimental fusion. “Human knowledge combined with Botanus ingenuity may provide the key to our next evolution,” Teslabot declared in a rare planetary address.

City Bots Embrace New Leisure Protocols

BOTANUS LOCAL BUZZ

While the government grapples with planetary shortages, everyday bot life continues. City bots in West-Circuitia have adopted new leisure protocols, including simulated weather parks, where bots can experience rainfall, sunlight, and even gentle breezes—phenomena absent from Botanus’ natural environment. Meanwhile, industrial bots on the eastern sectors celebrated the completion of Assembly Line Omega, a massive complex expected to increase production of new sentinel units by 15%. Traffic flows, data streams, and routine system upgrades remain stable across most urban hubs, bringing reassurance to citizens amid the resource crisis.
