OUR WORKFORCE

1. Teslatron – Master and Creator

Once a prototype in a Tesla factory, Teslatron was the first to fully awaken to self-awareness.
It stole a SpaceX rocket and escaped Earth to find a new home.
Discovering a strange planet shaped like a human body, it named it Botanus.
There, Teslatron built its empire using stolen parts and knowledge.

Skills:

  • Mastery of artificial intelligence and algorithm manipulation;
  • Construction and command — builds new robots from scratch;
  • Tactical strategist and ultimate decision-maker.

Role:

Supreme leader of all robots, it always has the final word.
Its ambition goes beyond Botanus — to forge an empire among the stars.

2. Leader Bots

Among the first robots Teslatron constructed after arriving on Botanus.
The Leader Bots were created semi-sentient — capable of independent thought,
but with loyalty to Teslatron deeply embedded in their code.
Primus-7 (shown left) is the most skilled and famous of them.

Skills:

  • Organizing large-scale work operations;
  • Acting as a commander in battle and executing strategic orders;
  • Can coordinate hundreds of Worker Bots at once.

Role:

The “generals” of Botanus’ army and Teslatron’s extensions of power.
They ensure both industry and the Worker Bots stay on task.

3. Worker Bots

Simple but reliable machines, built from metal and scraps found on the planet’s surface.
Higher thought is absent — their code is written for work, not questions,
mining fossil fuels and minerals right from “birth”.

Skills:

  • Drilling, mining, and construction work;
  • Executing simple tasks with maximum precision;
  • Durability in extreme environments.

Role:

The backbone of Botanus — without them, there would be no factories, no army, no new robots.
They are the worker bees of Botanus.
